## üéØ **Understanding Signals**

### **What Are Signals?**

Signals are AI-generated trading recommendations based on comprehensive market analysis. Each signal includes:

- **Direction**: YES/NO recommendation
- **Probability**: Likelihood of outcome (0-1)
- **Edge**: Mathematical advantage over market odds
- **Confidence**: Signal reliability score (0.1-1.0)
- **Position Size**: Recommended trade size
- **Rationale**: Detailed explanation of the analysis

### **Signal Quality Indicators**

**üü¢ High Confidence (0.8+)**
- Strong analytical foundation
- Multiple confirming factors
- Recommended for most traders

**üü° Medium Confidence (0.6-0.8)**
- Good analytical basis
- Some uncertainty factors
- Suitable for experienced traders

**üî¥ Low Confidence (0.4-0.6)**
- Limited supporting data
- Higher uncertainty
- For aggressive traders only

### **How to Use Signals**

1. **Review Signal Details**: Read the full rationale
2. **Check Confidence**: Ensure it matches your risk tolerance
3. **Verify Position Size**: Adjust based on your portfolio
4. **Execute Trade**: Use your preferred platform
5. **Monitor**: Track performance and market changes
